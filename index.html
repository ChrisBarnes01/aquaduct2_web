Platform

Our goal is to protect the environment by radically accelerating the world's transition to sustainable hydroponic agriculture.

Homes become Farms

We equip your home's unused space, like rooftops, with hydroponic technology that allows it to grow plants without the use of traditional farming methods.

We grow plants for you

Using machine learning, we remotely monitor the growth of your plants, provide maintainence, and enable the plants to thrive. The plants consume minimal water, and unlike traditional methods don't cause pollution to the land or require extensive deforrestation

Aggregrate and Distribute

At the conclusion of a growing cycle, we'll come and collect the grown plants for you, aggregrate the plants with the plants of your neighbor, and distribute them to local produce stores. Afterwards, we'll pay you for the plants you've grown.

Timeline

What does Aquaduct look like for you?

  • Step 1.

    Home Survey

    If you're Interested in starting the process of growing food, we'll conduct a survey of your buildings's growing spaces for free. In the survey, we'll determine the best crops to grow in your area, provide an estimated installation timeline, and calculate how much money you can expect to earn through your farm on a regular basis

  • Step 2.

    Installation Process

    After your building has been surveyed, we'll send a team to install a hydroponic system on your home. The installation is minimally invasive, and can be undone at any time. The designs are modular, aesthetically appealing, and can exist in a variety of building configurations - including on angled rooftops and on rooftops which already utilize solar panels. For this step, you can either pay up-front, or have the cost subtracted from your first few crop yeilds (for a net-zero out of pocket cost

  • Step 3.

    Plant Growth

    After the hydroponic system is installed, the growing process can begin. Starting out, our systems are specialized to grow profitable crops such as strawberries and lettuce. Using internal monitoring systems, the hydronic system will ensure that the crops recieve enough water, sunlight, and nutrients, and will keep you informed of plant progress and problems through the app. Since most processes are automated, you won't need to do much at all.

  • Step 4.

    Harvest

    Your plants are finished growing, we'll round together and collect them. We'll aggregrate your plants with the plants of other nearby growers, and then sell them together to local produce stores. You'll then be delivered a majority of the profit from the sales throgh the app, and your system will be provided new seeds. For plants like lettuce and straweberries, one growing cycle is approximately 30 days - meaning that you're set to earn money throughout the year.

Environmental Impacts of Aquaduct

Water Usage

Unlike traditional farms, we use precision technology to ensure that each plant only gets the water that it needs, and conserve the rest for other purposes.

Land Conservation

Our product was galvanized when we saw current agricultural processes burning down the Amazon. Our process only utilizes existing land.

Pollution

Unlike most farms, we do not create run-off pollution. In addition, we aspire to have all plastic systems come from reclaimed ocean plastic.
